Onyx: a sensation, a celebration, a magic of taste

ONYX

A fine gastronomic break

Opening this summer 2024, the Onyx restaurant is the new gastronomic flagship of the family-run Marki & CO group, behind the Passionné, Sphère and Shiro restaurants... successful addresses acclaimed by the gastronomic press and customer ratings on the internet.

The ONYX welcomes you in a remarkable setting that will transport you for the duration of a meal into the atmosphere of chic Parisian brasseries.

discover the menu

A gastronomy of emotions where the combination of flavors takes on its full meaning.

Design lovers

ONYX welcomes you to its two-storey premises, imagined and designed by the Anégil architectural firm, whose touch and sensibility are sublimated in this new address.

Each area of the restaurant has its own style and signature:

The ground floor is more conducive to business lunches, with its bright, airy atmosphere and wood-colored tones. The first level is cosier and more intimate, with a private lounge ideal for small meetings. Every visit to ONYX is punctuated by a climb up the elaborate metal staircase along the 4-meter-high Onyx marble wall.
